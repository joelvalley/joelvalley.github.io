Financial Restructuring

A financial restructuring engagement focused on stabilizing cash flow, optimizing capital structure, and restoring investor confidence during a period of operational transition.

Overview

We conducted a full balance-sheet review, modeled multiple restructuring scenarios, and worked closely with leadership to negotiate terms with creditors and align the organization on a sustainable path forward.

The Challenge

Rising debt obligations and declining margins created liquidity pressure. Leadership needed actionable options without disrupting day-to-day operations.

The Solution

The restructuring plan consolidated non-core assets, renegotiated debt covenants, and introduced monthly financial dashboards for real-time visibility into performance.
